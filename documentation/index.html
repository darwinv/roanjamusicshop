What is this?

This is a module that allows you to add mp3 previews to your album page or mp3 product page(it lets the user to listen a sample mp3 before purchasing the real product). It also allows you to add a preview mp3 from other products and it adds the price of the product and a 'buy' button.

Features

  • Add mp3 previews for each product.
  • You can associate an mp3 file from another product.
  • Automatically gets the price from the associated product and from the current product.
  • Module supports two hook placements for your mp3 previews on product page.
  • Very easy to use.
  • It supports the latest prestashop versions(1.5.6.2 - 1.6.0.9).

 

How does it work?

How to install the module:

1.Go to your admin panel.
2.Go to modules->modules .
3.Press the 'Add a new module' at the top of the modules page.
4.Upload the .zip file with the module provided('Prestashop Music Shop').

How to use the module?

After installing it, you can select where you would like to hook your mp3 previews on the product pages:
 
1.Go to your admin panel.
2.Go to modules->modules .
3.Search for 'Prestashop Music Shop'  and press the 'configure' button.
4.Select where you would like your mp3 previews to appear on the page and press the 'Save' button.
 

How to add mp3 previews to your product pages:

ATTENTION!!!!IN PRESTASHOP VERSION 1.5.X.X PRESS THE 'SAVE BUTTON' and not the 'SAVE AND STAY' button, it may not save your DATA!!!!
1.Go to your admin panel
2.Go to Catalog->Products
3.Select your product from the list and press the 'edit' button.
4.You will notice that a new tab appeared(on the left side panel), named 'Music Shop Module By Rapty', click on this tab.
5.Here you can add your mp3's for your product. Here you can associate your product with another one, if you do this, add an mp3 file that represents the associated product(For example you would like to add a sample mp3 from another product to your currently viewing product.)
6.Press 'Save and stay' after adding your new mp3.
 
Voila, now you can check your product to see if the mp3 previews, buy buttons and price were added.
 
Please note that the prices are shown without any reduction.
In PRESTASHOP 1.6.0.9 there's a major bug and will be fixed in 1.6.0.11 - http://forge.prestashop.com/browse/PSCSX-1257 - Please wait several seconds(wait untill the arrows on the top left side of the admin panel finish rotating) untill you save your MP3 files otherwise it may reset your virtual product's data.
